I was born in Salta, Argentina. At the age of 18, I moved to Buenos Aires to study at the Universidad del Cine, and since then, I have been dedicated to developing my film and literary projects. I also work in sound design. I have directed three short films: El patio (2020), winner of the Special Jury Mention at PLATÓ and Argentine finalist at FACIUNI; Sarfo (2023), a documentary following a racehorse; and in 2024, I directed and produced Río Blanco (2024), a fiction short film that follows a hunter during a storm on a hill, thanks to the support of the IDEA funding program from the province of Salta. It was the winner of the Ibero-American Short Film Competition at FIC.UBA. I am currently developing my first feature film. On this site, you will find fragments of my work, links to personally relevant sites, as well as images and texts authored by me.
